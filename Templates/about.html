Parakh

Governance Evaluation & Intelligence Platform

Our Commitment

Parakh is committed to strengthening digital governance through continuous evaluation, transparency, and data-driven decision-making. The platform is designed to assess data quality, accessibility, service coverage, and systemic risks to ensure inclusive and accountable public service delivery.

Our Activities

  • Analyzing enrolment and update trends across regions and demographics
  • Identifying under-served populations and infrastructure gaps
  • Detecting biometric failures and accessibility challenges
  • Flagging anomalous patterns and potential misuse of systems
  • Generating evidence-based insights to support policy formulation

Our Impact

By transforming raw operational data into actionable intelligence, Parakh enables institutions to optimize service distribution, improve system reliability, reduce exclusion risks, and enhance overall governance effectiveness.

Our Vision

To build a trusted analytical framework that empowers institutions to evaluate digital public infrastructure objectively, promote fairness, and ensure that technology-driven governance reaches every citizen equitably.
